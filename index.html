TapLogBaby is a mobile app designed to help parents easily track their baby's daily activities, including feedings, diaper changes, sleep patterns, and more.

Features

  • Track feedings (breastfeeding, bottle, solids)
  • Log diaper changes
  • Monitor sleep patterns
  • Sync data across devices with Google or Apple sign-in
